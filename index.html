Our Mission to Help Others

Care Coin Crypto started as an initiative to help charities fundraise money. We saw gaps between people who wanted to make a difference and people who indulge in the crypto world. With Care Coin, you can accomplish these two lifestyles. How is this possible? Care Coin is reserving 25% of the total supply of tokens to donate in the future to a variety of charities. Your involvement with Care Coin will indirectly benefit those in need.

We hope that Care Coin becomes a token of appreciation to people in our lives. When someone receives a Care Coin token, they will know that they are loved and appreciated. Help us make this mission a success by spreading the word and spreading the Care.
